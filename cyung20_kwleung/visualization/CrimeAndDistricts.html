The above map is a visualization of the crime in the districts in Boston based on crime reports from August 2015-
Each shaded area is a district. More crime-infested districts are colored darker. Feel free to click on the districts to see the district name and # of crimes from August 2015-forward.
Likewise, click on the icons showing police badges which represents to find out the particularly name of the Boston Police District Station located on where the icon is placed
